About

Jason Xuan is an architect and designer, founder of NUO Workshop, originally from Ningbo, Zhejiang, China. Currently pursuing his Master of Architecture 2 degree at Harvard University's Graduate School of Design (GSD).

Jason's design research follows two key trajectories. The first focuses on building in the real world, with particular attention to sustainable construction and materiality. The second branch explores the virtual world, delving into digital spaces and immersive experiences. His design interests are centered on themes of "reality and hyper-reality," aiming to bridge physical and digital environments, reflecting his broader commitment to creating adaptive, sustainable, and deeply contextual architecture that resonates with both craftsmanship and emerging technologies.
